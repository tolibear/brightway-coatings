# Brightway Coatings Website Copy Project

## Background and Motivation

Create high-converting, trust-first marketing copy for Brightway Coatings - a family-owned concrete coatings company in Jacksonville, FL. The project requires developing comprehensive website content that:
- Generates qualified leads through calls and quote requests
- Builds trust through family story and craftsmanship proof
- Educates on polyaspartic benefits without technical jargon
- Mirrors the modern, cinematic feel of swimmingpools.be
- Maintains fast, mobile-first, SEO-optimized performance

## Key Challenges and Analysis

1. **Brand Voice Balance**: Must be warm and trustworthy without being salesy, professional yet approachable
2. **Technical Education**: Explaining polyaspartic advantages over epoxy in plain English
3. **Local SEO**: Targeting specific Jacksonville area neighborhoods while maintaining natural copy flow
4. **Conversion Optimization**: Placing CTAs strategically without being pushy
5. **Content Volume**: 9 pages with 7,000+ total words requiring consistent voice
6. **Trust Building**: Conveying 30+ years experience and family values authentically

## Lessons

### Technical Lessons
- Always use hyphen instead of em dash in copy
- Maintain NAP consistency across all mentions
- Keep hero videos under 6MB for performance

### Content Lessons  
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use specific neighborhood names for local SEO
- Avoid generic terms like "best" - use specifics instead
- Keep sentences short and use strong verbs

### Brand Voice Guidelines
- Warm, plain-English, confident, service-minded
- Family-owned values - trustworthy, meticulous, never salesy
- Short sentences, strong verbs, concrete outcomes
- American spelling throughout
